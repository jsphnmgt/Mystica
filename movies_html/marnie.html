When Marnie Was There

Anna Sasaki, a quiet and isolated girl suffering from frequent asthma attacks, is sent to the countryside by her foster parent in hopes that the cleaner air will improve her health. While staying with her aunt and uncle, Anna explores her passion for sketching and discovers an abandoned mansion called the Marsh House. There, she meets a mysterious girl named Marnie, whose lively nature helps Anna break out of her shell. As their friendship deepens, Anna begins to uncover hidden truths about Marnie and the mansion, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and unforgettable memories.
