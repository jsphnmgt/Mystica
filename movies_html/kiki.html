Kiki's Delivery Service

Kiki, a 13-year-old witch-in-training, embarks on a year-long journey to become a full-fledged witch by living alone in the coastal town of Koriko. Leaving her family and friends behind, she flies on her broomstick with her black cat Jiji but initially struggles to find her footing. After wandering the streets, she meets Osono, who offers her a place to stay in exchange for helping with deliveries for her bakery. Inspired, Kiki launches her own broomstick courier service, navigating her new responsibilities while discovering her place among the townsfolk and the meaning of independence.
