Howl's Moving Castle

In a small town, Sophie Hatter, the plain daughter of a hatmaker, leads a quiet life until a chance encounter with the infamous wizard Howl changes everything. After rescuing her from a perilous situation, Howl unwittingly causes the vengeful Witch of the Waste to curse Sophie, turning her into an old woman. Determined to break the curse, Sophie joins Howl and his eccentric companions—including a fire demon and a hopping scarecrow—on an adventurous journey in Howl’s moving castle, all while a war threatens to tear their kingdom apart.
