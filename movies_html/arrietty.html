The Secret World of Arrietty

During a summer at his aunt's house, the young and ailing Shou discovers Arrietty, a miniature girl living with her parents, Pod and Homily, in the basement. Known as "Borrowers," they survive by quietly taking small items from humans. Despite the dangers of being discovered and the risks of venturing into human spaces, Arrietty befriends Shou, sparking events that may change their lives. As their friendship deepens, it bridges the divide between their two worlds, despite the challenges and differences they face.
